Human Impact and Land Managment

Human Impact on New Zealand

Once the Maoris arrived at New Zealand they brough lots and non native things with them. Animals, plants, etc. They also burnt down lots of forest to clear land and build villages. The Maoris also intorduced a number of species that made lots of other animals become extinct. On top of all of this the Europeans also came to New Zealand and had there on impacts on New Zealand

Land Managment in New Zealand

The way the Maoris managed their land was through their social heirachy. The supreme cheif was at the top of this heirachy so he would get the most land and decide who else got what land. They would also decide the restrictions and rules around using that land.

Human Impact on Easter Island

Easter Island used to be a lush island filled with trees and an abundance of life. This all went away because as the population of easter island grew, the resources needed also increased. This ment all the trees and resources were used up until there was none left.

Land Managment in Easter Island

The Easter Islanders would base the land management of their social heirachy as well. The cheif would have the most land and dictate the where the rest of the land went.

Easter Island vs New Zealand

Both of these islands used simlar land managment stategies that both were based off the social heirachy. They both got rid of their resources but for diffrent reasons.
