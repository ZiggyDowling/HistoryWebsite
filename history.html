The people of Easter island were called the Rapu Nui. The first Rapu Nui to settle there were in the year 1000. Once some time pased more polonesians came to settle on Easter Island. 200 years after the first settlers arrived the civilation started to expand. Houses were built with an abundance of nature all around the island. But as the Island grew in population the resources of the island started to depelet rapidly. The first European contact to Easter Island was in the year 1722. A Dutch explorer was called Jacob Roggeveen. In the years to come more explorers such as the English James cook also came to Easter Island. Lots more infomation about Easter Island has been lost to time.
